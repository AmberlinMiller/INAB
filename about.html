    ABOUT

    It's Not A Box Theatre is a digital immersive theatre company dedicated to devising interactive works that invites stories from unheard voices, subverts the relationship between audience and artist, and experiments with what it truly means to play.

    MANDATE

    We believe all art is both personal and political.We believe radical work comes from radical artists, which for us means perspectives and practices forged from our intersections of identities, shared oppression, and visions for the future. It also means that, as our Artistic Directorship is 100% LGBTQ and 50% BIPOC, we are committed to assembling project teams that reflect this demographic.

    We manifest works and spaces that honour accessibility and reflect our class conscious, anti-capitalist, anti-patriarchal, anti-colonial, and anti-ableist values. We know there is a lot of injustice and that a lot of people do not agree with us. We are not here to change your mind. We are here to change your reality.
